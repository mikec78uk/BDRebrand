The strategic shift

From broad portfolio
to pure-play medtech

Our shift from a broad portfolio to pure-play medtech enhances our focus and strengthens our ability to support your success.

2015–2017
Major acquisitions establish BD's scale
CareFusion ($12.2B) and C.R. Bard ($24B) make BD a global medtech giant — with leading positions in infusion, medication management, and interventional surgery. Establishing significant scale, laying the groundwork for future-focused impact.
Broad scope
2020
BD 2025 strategy: Grow, Simplify, Empower
CEO Tom Polen launches a deliberate simplification programme. A deliberate simplification programme, intentionally sharpening our focus on high-growth markets to empower our partners and anticipate tomorrow's demands.
Simplification begins
February 2025
Biosciences and diagnostics separation announced
BD's board unanimously authorises the separation of its Biosciences and Diagnostic Solutions business. Two focused companies, each better positioned to lead within their sectors, are empowering greater clarity and innovation for the healthcare ecosystem.
Two companies announced
2026 — Now
The new BD: pure-play medtech
The separation is complete. BD is now a focused, pure-play medical technology company — structured around four clear segments, investing concentrated capital in high-growth medtech, built on a model that ensures predictable impact and allows us to courageously define the future's trajectory.
